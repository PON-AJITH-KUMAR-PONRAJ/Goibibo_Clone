About Goibibo.com

Goibibo is one of India’s leading online travel booking brands, providing a range of booking choices for hotels, flights, trains, buses, and cars for travelers. Through its mobile app and website, travelers can search, plan, and book a wide range of travel services and products in India as well as overseas. Goibibo’s core value differentiator is its most trusted user experience, be it in terms of the quickest search and booking, fastest payments, settlements, or refund processes.

Management Team

Ankit Khanna

Chief Product Officer – Strategic Initiatives & New Business

Ankit is Chief Product Officer and is responsible for the Online Product development for Strategic Initiatives and New Businesses. Ankit brings in 15 years of rich experience across industries. Previously, he was the Head of Product at CareemPAY based out of UAE. Prior to joining CareemPAY, Ankit was the Chief Operating Officer and Chief Product Officer for Freecharge and was looking after merchant acquisition, user growth & marketing & operations. He had joined the Snapdeal’s founding team at its inception in 2010 as the first product professional. Ankit also co-founded itsaver in 2006, an online service for photo printing. Ankit is a graduate from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi.

Parikshit Choudhry

Chief Business Officer – Ground Transport

Parikshit Choudhry is the Chief Business Officer – Ground Transport. In his current role, he is responsible for driving growth and revenue for the company’s ground transport category including cabs, metro, and rail. Prior to this, he was spearheading the company’s domestic hotel supply category.

Prior to joining Goibibo, Parikshit was associated with magicbricks.com where he was responsible for setting up the Sales Force Effectiveness (SFE) function from scratch. His other stints include GE Capital where he was nominated for GE's prestigious Crotonville Leadership Training Programs – Foundations of GE leadership and Building Essential Leadership Skills, MRF Tyres, and HDFC Bank.
